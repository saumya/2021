A long wait is finally over.

For a long long time I was lazy and was avoiding the work. But here it is.

Somehow things are moving in a different direction. That made me think, if I am going to do it anyway, why not now. So the upgrade. And with this, I have released the theme(GoWalk) of this site as opensource. It is simple and open, customise it and use it, if you want.

Have a happy life.
